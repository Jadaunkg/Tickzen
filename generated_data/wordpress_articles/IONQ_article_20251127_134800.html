IonQ's Core Business: Powering the Quantum Revolution

IonQ, Inc. is dedicated to developing advanced quantum computers and networks, primarily operating within the United States. Its core business involves selling access to its quantum computers, which are available in various qubit capacities.

What's particularly compelling is how IonQ makes its quantum computing resources accessible. They partner with major cloud platforms like Amazon Web Services (AWS) Amazon Braket, Microsoft's Azure Quantum, and Google's Cloud Marketplace. This broad accessibility, coupled with its own cloud service, positions IonQ to serve a wide range of researchers and enterprises.

Beyond direct quantum computing access, IonQ is also engaged in other critical areas of quantum technology. This includes quantum-safe networking and quantum detection systems, offering products like quantum key distribution systems, quantum random number generators, and single-photon detectors. The company also provides contracts for the design, development, construction, and sale of specialized quantum computing hardware, alongside maintenance, support, and consulting services for co-developing algorithms.

Notably, IonQ has a collaboration agreement with AstraZeneca PLC, focusing on the development of quantum-accelerated computational chemistry workflows for healthcare, life sciences, and chemistry. Founded in 2015 and headquartered in College Park, Maryland, IonQ is steadily building its footprint in this transformative industry.
