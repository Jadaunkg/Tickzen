Airbnb's Core Business Model Explained

To truly understand ABNB's potential, we need to grasp its core business. Airbnb, Inc., along with its various subsidiaries, operates a global platform that connects hosts with guests. This platform allows hosts to offer unique stays and experiences to travelers all over the world. Whether it's through their website or mobile devices, guests can easily book everything from a cozy room to an entire home, or even participate in local experiences. The company also offers gift cards, expanding its reach and revenue streams. Originally known as AirBed & Breakfast, Inc., the company officially changed its name to Airbnb, Inc. in November 2010. Founded in 2007, Airbnb is headquartered in the vibrant city of San Francisco, California. Their innovative approach has truly revolutionized the hospitality industry.
