Business Overview

Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company Limited, along with its various subsidiaries, is a global leader in the manufacturing, packaging, testing, and sale of integrated circuits and other semiconductor devices. They operate across Taiwan, China, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, Japan, the United States, and many other international markets.

TSM offers a wide array of wafer fabrication processes. This includes advanced technologies for manufacturing complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor (CMOS) logic, mixed-signal, radio frequency, embedded memory, bipolar CMOS mixed-signal, and various other specialized processes. Essentially, they are the backbone for countless technological innovations worldwide.

Beyond manufacturing, the company also provides extensive customer and engineering support services, manufactures masks, and actively invests in technology start-up companies. They are also involved in the research, design, development, packaging, testing, and sale of color filters. TSM's products are absolutely critical components used in high-performance computing, the latest smartphones, Internet of Things (IoT) devices, automotive electronics, and digital consumer electronics.

Incorporated in 1987, TSM has grown into an indispensable player in the global technology supply chain, headquartered in Hsinchu City, Taiwan. Their reach and impact are truly global, making them a fascinating company to analyze for any investor.
