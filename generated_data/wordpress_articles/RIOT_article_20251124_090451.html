Riot Platforms, Inc. Business Description

Riot Platforms, Inc., along with its subsidiaries, operates as a prominent Bitcoin mining company primarily within the United States. Its core operations are split into two key segments: Bitcoin Mining and Engineering.

The company provides comprehensive and critical infrastructure for institutional-scale Bitcoin mining facilities. You'll find these large-scale operations in Rockdale and Navarro counties, Texas, as well as two additional Bitcoin mining sites located in Paducah, Kentucky.

Beyond mining, Riot Platforms also focuses on engineering. This segment designs and manufactures power distribution equipment and custom-engineered electrical products. They also offer electricity distribution product design, manufacturing, and installation services.

Their engineering services cater to a wide range of clients, including large-scale commercial and governmental customers, as well as those in data center, power generation, utility, water, industrial, and alternative energy markets. Founded in 2000, Riot Platforms, Inc. is headquartered in Castle Rock, Colorado.
