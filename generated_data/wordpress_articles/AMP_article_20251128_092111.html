Understanding Ameriprise Financial's Core Business Segments

Ameriprise Financial, Inc., along with its various subsidiaries, operates as a comprehensive financial services provider, catering to individual and institutional clients across the United States and internationally. The company strategically divides its operations into three primary segments. The Advice & Wealth Management segment is where clients receive financial planning and advice. This segment offers a broad array of services including brokerage products, discretionary and non-discretionary investment advisory accounts, mutual funds, insurance and annuities, and cash management solutions. Next up is the Asset Management segment, which operates under the well-known Columbia Threadneedle Investments brand. This segment delivers investment management, advice, and products to a diverse client base, including retail, high net worth, and institutional clients. Their offerings span U.S. and non-U.S. mutual funds, exchange-traded funds (ETFs), variable product funds, and a suite of institutional asset management products like separately managed accounts, collateralized loan obligations (CLOs), and hedge funds. Finally, the Retirement & Protection Solutions segment focuses on providing variable annuity products, as well as life and disability income insurance products, primarily to retail clients. This diversified structure allows Ameriprise to serve a wide range of client needs across their financial lifecycle.
