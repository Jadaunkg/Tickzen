CrowdStrike's Core Business & Innovation

CrowdStrike Holdings, Inc. is a leading provider of cybersecurity solutions, serving customers across the United States and internationally. Their core offering is a unified platform that delivers cloud-based protection for endpoints, cloud workloads, identity, and data. They operate primarily through a software-as-a-service (SaaS) subscription model, which provides a recurring revenue stream.

The company's extensive suite of services includes corporate endpoint and cloud workload security, managed security, vulnerability management, IT operations management, identity protection, and threat intelligence. They also offer data protection, SaaS security posture management, and even AI-powered workflow automation and generative AI workload security services. Essentially, they provide a comprehensive security orchestration, automation, and response (SOAR) platform, along with security information and event management (SIEM) and log management services. Founded in 2011 and headquartered in Austin, Texas, CrowdStrike has firmly established itself as an innovator in the cybersecurity space.
