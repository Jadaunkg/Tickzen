CHPT Business Overview: Powering the EV Revolution

ChargePoint Holdings, Inc., along with its subsidiaries, is a key player in the electric vehicle (EV) charging infrastructure. They provide comprehensive EV charging networks and solutions across both North America and Europe, which is a massive and growing market.

The company caters to a diverse range of customers. This includes commercial entities like retail establishments, workplaces, hospitality venues, healthcare facilities, fueling and convenience stores, and parking lot operators. They also serve the fleet market, which encompasses municipal buses, delivery and work vehicles, as well as port, airport, warehouse, and other industrial applications, alongside ride-sharing services.

Furthermore, ChargePoint extends its services to residential customers, covering both single-family homes and multi-family apartments and condominiums. Founded in 2007, ChargePoint Holdings, Inc. is headquartered in Campbell, California, positioning itself at the heart of the tech and innovation landscape.
