HIMS Business Overview: What Does Hims & Hers Health Do?

Hims & Hers Health, Inc. operates a telehealth platform that connects consumers with licensed healthcare professionals. They serve customers not just in the United States but also across the United Kingdom and other international markets. This broad reach is a significant part of their growth strategy. The company offers a diverse portfolio of curated prescription and non-prescription health and wellness products and services. These are conveniently available for purchase directly through their websites and mobile application. They provide recurring prescription medication services, along with ongoing care from healthcare providers. Beyond prescriptions, HIMS also offers a variety of over-the-counter drugs, devices, cosmetics, and supplements. These products primarily focus on general wellness, sexual health, skincare, and hair care. For instance, their wellness specialty category includes products like melatonin and biotin. In skincare, you'll find moisturizers, creams, sunscreens, and face oils. Sexual health offerings range from condoms to vibrators, while hair care includes shampoos, conditioners, and topical treatments like minoxidil. What's more, Hims & Hers Health provides medical consultation and post-consultation support services. They also distribute health and wellness products through various wholesale partners, expanding their market footprint. The company is also innovating with "Labs," a service that measures key health markers over time and offers doctor-developed action plans. Hims & Hers Health, Inc. is headquartered in San Francisco, California.
