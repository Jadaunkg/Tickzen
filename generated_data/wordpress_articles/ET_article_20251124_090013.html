Business Overview: What Drives Energy Transfer LP?

Energy Transfer LP, along with its subsidiaries, is a behemoth in the energy sector, providing a vast array of energy-related services across the United States. The company's operations are segmented, encompassing everything from transportation and storage to processing and marketing. They own and operate an extensive network of natural gas transportation pipelines and storage facilities, including approximately 12,200 miles of intrastate and 20,090 miles of interstate natural gas pipelines. Beyond just transportation, Energy Transfer LP also sells natural gas to a diverse client base, including electric utilities, independent power plants, local distribution companies, and industrial end-users. The company's midstream activities are comprehensive, involving natural gas gathering pipelines, processing plants, and facilities for treating and conditioning natural gas. They also manage natural gas gathering, oil pipeline, and oil stabilization facilities. Furthermore, Energy Transfer LP boasts an impressive 5,700 miles of Natural Gas Liquid (NGL) pipelines, NGL fractionation and storage facilities, and other NGL storage assets and terminals. In the crude oil space, they handle transportation, terminalling, acquisition, and marketing activities, operating approximately 17,950 miles of crude oil trunk and gathering pipelines. The company also sells and distributes motor fuels and other petroleum products under well-known brands like Sunoco and EcoMaxx. Their services extend to natural gas compression, wholesale power trading, and even carbon dioxide and hydrogen sulfide removal. Beyond these, Energy Transfer LP manages coal and natural resources properties, sells standing timber, leases coal-related infrastructure, and collects oil and gas royalties. It’s a truly diversified portfolio within the energy infrastructure. Originally known as Energy Transfer Equity, L.P., the company rebranded to Energy Transfer LP in October 2018. Founded in 1996, it maintains its headquarters in Dallas, Texas. For more detailed information, you can always check their official SEC filings at sec.gov.
