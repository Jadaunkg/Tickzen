Rivian Automotive, Inc., along with its subsidiaries, is at the forefront of designing, developing, manufacturing, and selling electric vehicles and their associated accessories. Headquartered in Irvine, California, and founded in 2009, Rivian has quickly made a name for itself in the competitive EV space. The company's offerings include popular consumer vehicles like the two-row, five-passenger pickup truck under the R1T brand, and the three-row, seven-passenger sport utility vehicle known as the R1S. The company's direct-to-consumer sales model is a key differentiator in both the consumer and commercial markets.
