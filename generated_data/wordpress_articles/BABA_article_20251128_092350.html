Alibaba Business Overview

Alibaba Group Holding Limited, through its vast network of subsidiaries, provides essential technology infrastructure and extensive marketing reach. This empowers merchants, brands, retailers, and other businesses to effectively engage with their users and customers, both within the People's Republic of China and internationally.

The company's core operations include digital retail platforms like Taobao and Tmall, alongside wholesale marketplaces such as 1688.com and Alibaba.com. For global reach, it operates AliExpress, Lazada, Trendyol, and Daraz, making it a significant player in cross-border e-commerce.

Beyond retail, Alibaba's ecosystem is incredibly diverse. It includes Cainiao, a sophisticated e-commerce logistics solution; Ele.me, an on-demand delivery and local services platform; and Amap, a leading provider of mobile digital map, navigation, and real-time traffic information. The company also has a strong presence in entertainment with Youku, an online video platform, and Damai Entertainment, which handles content production, ticketing, and event management.

Furthermore, Alibaba Cloud offers a comprehensive suite of cloud computing services, encompassing elastic computing, storage, network, security, database, big data, and cloud-native solutions. They also provide hardware, software licensing, and application development services.

Rounding out its diverse portfolio are Freshippo for groceries, Alibaba Health for medical solutions, Lingxi Games for mobile gaming, UC Browser and Quark for information services, Fliggy for online travel, and DingTalk for enterprise collaboration. Founded in 1999, Alibaba Group Holding Limited is headquartered in Causeway Bay, Hong Kong.
