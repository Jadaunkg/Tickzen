Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI stock analysis platform.

How accurate are the AI stock predictions?

Our AI stock predictions utilize Facebook Prophet's time-series forecasting model, which has demonstrated strong accuracy in predicting general market trends. The platform achieves an average accuracy rate of 70-85% for short-term forecasts (1-7 days) and 65-75% for medium-term forecasts (8-30 days). However, it's important to note that no prediction system can guarantee 100% accuracy due to market volatility and unforeseen events. We recommend using our predictions as one component of a comprehensive investment strategy.

What data sources do you use?

Our platform sources data from multiple reliable financial data providers. We use YFinance for historical stock price data, company fundamentals, and options data. Macroeconomic indicators are pulled from FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data) using pandas-datareader. All data is processed and validated before being used in our analysis and forecasting models to ensure accuracy and reliability.

How do I publish reports to my WordPress site?

Our WordPress publishing feature is available on Professional and Enterprise plans. To set it up, you'll need to add your WordPress site details in the Site Profile Management section, including your site URL, WordPress username, and application password. Once configured, you can schedule automatic publishing or manually trigger the publication of reports. The system supports custom post categories, featured images, and can distribute posts among multiple authors if needed.

Which markets and stocks do you support?

Our platform supports stocks from major global markets including the NYSE, NASDAQ, AMEX, TSX (Canada), LSE (UK), Euronext, Frankfurt Exchange, ASX (Australia), and major Asian markets including Tokyo, Hong Kong, and Shanghai. We cover over 100,000 stocks globally, as well as ETFs, mutual funds, and some cryptocurrencies. If you need analysis for a specific market not listed here, please contact our support team.

Can I customize the reports?

Yes, you can customize reports in several ways. In the Site Profile Management section, you can select which sections to include in your reports (company profile, valuation metrics, technical analysis, forecasts, etc.). Enterprise users get additional customization options including custom CSS styling, logo placement, and white-labeling capabilities. You can also configure the tone and formatting of the written analysis to match your brand's voice.
