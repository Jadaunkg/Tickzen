Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Tickzen's AI-powered stock analysis platform.

What is Tickzen? +

Tickzen is an AI-powered stock analysis platform that helps investors make informed decisions. We provide technical analysis, fundamental analysis, stock screening, portfolio tracking, and market news to give you a comprehensive view of the market and individual stocks. Our platform combines advanced machine learning algorithms with financial data to identify trends and patterns that might not be immediately apparent to human analysts. Learn more on our About page.

Is Tickzen offering financial advice? +

No, Tickzen does not provide personalized financial advice. The information, analysis, and tools we provide are for informational and research purposes only. Our platform helps you analyze financial data and market trends, but the final investment decision should be made by you, possibly in consultation with a qualified financial advisor who understands your personal financial situation and goals. For more details, please review our Terms of Service and Disclaimer.

How does Tickzen's AI technology work? +

Tickzen uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze vast amounts of financial data, including price movements, trading volumes, company fundamentals, financial statements, market news, and social media sentiment. Our AI models identify patterns, correlations, and anomalies that might indicate investment opportunities or risks. The technology continuously learns from new data, improving its accuracy over time. Visit our Technology page for an in-depth explanation of our approach.

How accurate are Tickzen's predictions? +

While we strive for high accuracy in our analyses, it's important to understand that stock market predictions inherently involve uncertainty. Our AI models provide probabilistic assessments based on historical data and current market conditions, but cannot predict unforeseen events or market shocks. We recommend using our analyses as one of several tools in your investment decision-making process, not as the sole determinant.

Which markets does Tickzen cover? +

Tickzen primarily covers U.S. stock markets including NASDAQ, NYSE, and AMEX. We provide data and analysis for over 8,000 publicly traded companies. We're continuously expanding our coverage to include international markets and additional asset classes like ETFs, mutual funds, and cryptocurrencies. Check our Stock Coverage page to see the full list of supported stocks.

How does the WordPress integration work? +

Tickzen's WordPress integration allows you to automatically publish professionally formatted stock analysis content to your WordPress site. The process works as follows:

  1. Create a Publishing Profile by navigating to the Publishing Profile Dashboard
  2. Configure your WordPress connection by providing your site URL, WordPress user credentials, and other settings
  3. Select which report sections to include in your published content
  4. Go to the Run Automation page and select the ticker source (upload a file or manual entry)
  5. Start the automation process, which will generate and publish content according to your settings

The content is automatically formatted with proper headings, paragraphs, bullet points, and includes SEO optimization. You can connect up to 2 WordPress sites with the current free access, with more sites available in our upcoming paid plans. Visit our WordPress Integration Guide for detailed instructions.

How do I set up a WordPress Publishing Profile? +

Setting up a WordPress Publishing Profile is straightforward:

  1. Navigate to the Publishing Profile Dashboard in your account
  2. Click "Add New Publishing Profile"
  3. Fill in the required fields:
    • Profile Name: A name to identify this publishing destination
    • Site URL: Your WordPress website address
    • Default Google Sheet Name: For ticker lists (optional)
    • WordPress Category ID: Where posts will be published
    • Min/Max Scheduling Gap: Time between posts in minutes
  4. Add at least one WordPress writer with:
    • WordPress Username
    • WordPress User ID
    • WordPress Application Password
  5. Select which report sections to include in your published content
  6. Click "ADD PROFILE" to save your configuration

Once your profile is set up, you can use it to automate publishing to your WordPress site by going to the Run Automation page and selecting your publishing profile. For more detailed instructions, visit our WordPress Setup Guide.

How do I run the WordPress publishing automation? +

After setting up your Publishing Profile, you can run the automation process:

  1. Navigate to the Run Automation page for your profile
  2. Check your current settings including:
    • Your daily publishing quota (e.g., "Today: 8 / 15")
    • Current ticker file and processing status
  3. Select your ticker source:
    • Upload/Use Saved File: Use a CSV or Excel file with ticker symbols
    • Manual Entry: Enter specific tickers manually
  4. If using a file, you can upload a new one to replace the current file
  5. Start the automation process, which will:
    • Generate content for each ticker
    • Schedule posts according to your gap settings
    • Assign writers from your profile to each post
    • Publish directly to your WordPress site
  6. Monitor the Activity Log for real-time updates on the publishing process

The automation will process tickers according to your settings, spacing out publications to maintain a natural publishing cadence on your WordPress site. You can track progress in the Activity Log, which shows which tickers have been processed and which writer was assigned to each. For troubleshooting, visit our WordPress Troubleshooting Guide
