Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are Tickzen's stock predictions?

Our AI models are trained on extensive historical data and continuously refined. While no prediction can be 100% accurate, our forecasts consistently outperform traditional methods. We provide confidence intervals with all predictions and regularly publish accuracy metrics on our platform.

How frequently is your data updated?

We refresh market data daily after market close, ensuring our analysis includes the most recent trading information. Fundamental data is updated as companies release their financial statements, typically quarterly.

Do you offer API access to your analysis?

Yes, we offer API access for developers and businesses who want to integrate our AI stock analysis into their platforms. Please contact our partnerships team for details about pricing and integration support.

How can I request analysis for a specific stock?

Simply enter the ticker symbol in the analyzer tool on our platform. If we don't currently support a specific stock, you can request it through the platform, and we'll prioritize adding it to our database.
