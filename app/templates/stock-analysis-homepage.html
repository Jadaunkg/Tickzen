Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our AI-powered stock analysis platform, features, and investment research capabilities

How accurate are the AI stock predictions and technical analysis?
Our AI models analyze 15+ years of historical price data using advanced algorithms for RSI, MACD, moving averages, and pattern recognition. The forecast tables include confidence intervals based on proven technical indicators. While no analysis can predict markets with 100% accuracy, our users report significant improvement in their investment timing and decision-making process.
How does the WordPress automation and SEO optimization work?
Our smart publishing system features randomized scheduling, writer rotation, and automatic SEO optimization including meta descriptions, schema markup, and strategic internal linking. The system prevents spam detection by using natural publishing patterns and rotating through different author profiles with customizable posting intervals.
Can I customize which sections appear in my WordPress posts?
Yes! You have complete control over all 32 available sections including introduction, current market status, metrics summary, price forecast chart, detailed forecast table, company profile, total valuation, share statistics, valuation metrics, financial health, financial efficiency, profitability growth, dividends shareholder returns, technical analysis summary, bollinger bands chart, RSI chart, MACD charts, historical price & volume, historical performance, stock price statistics, quarterly earnings, short selling info, risk analysis, sentiment analysis, peer comparison, risk factors, analyst insights, recent news, macro economic context, investment recommendation, conclusion outlook, and FAQ. You can create different configurations for different sites and customize the content depth for each section to match your audience's needs.
What data sources do you use for the 15-year historical analysis?
We aggregate data from premium financial sources including Bloomberg, Reuters, Yahoo Finance, SEC filings, FRED economic data, and major exchanges. Our historical price tables and charts cover 15 years of data with daily, weekly, and monthly granularity for comprehensive trend analysis and technical indicator calculations.
How does the SEO optimization and content marketing work?
Every generated report includes stock ticker keyword optimization, 1-2 strategic external links to authoritative financial sources, proper meta descriptions, FAQ sections, and schema markup. The content is structured with H2/H3 headers and optimized for financial search terms to maximize organic visibility and search engine rankings.
What's included in the upcoming historical events analyzer?
This feature will analyze how stocks reacted to major historical events (earnings, splits, market crashes, economic announcements, etc.) and identify patterns in technical indicators during those periods. It will help predict how similar events might affect stock prices based on historical precedent and current market conditions.
Is the platform suitable for both beginner and professional investors?
Absolutely! Our platform provides comprehensive analysis that beginners can understand with clear explanations and risk assessments, while offering the depth and technical sophistication that professional investors and financial advisors require for institutional-grade research and decision-making.
How often is the stock data updated and how current are the analyses?
Our platform uses real-time market data feeds with updates throughout trading hours. Stock analyses are generated with the latest available data including current price, recent earnings, analyst revisions, and market sentiment. Technical indicators are calculated using the most recent trading sessions to ensure accuracy and relevance.
