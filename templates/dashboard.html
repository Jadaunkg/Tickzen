Our Tools & Techniques

At Tickzen, we leverage a combination of sophisticated AI models, comprehensive data sources, and robust analytical techniques to provide you with actionable insights. Our goal is to empower your investment decisions and streamline your content generation workflows.

  • AI-Powered Predictions: We primarily use time-series forecasting models like Facebook Prophet, enhanced with machine learning techniques (e.g., LSTM networks where applicable) to predict stock price movements. These models are trained on extensive historical price data.
  • Comprehensive Data Analysis:
    • Technical Analysis: Calculation and visualization of key indicators such as RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, Moving Averages, and identification of support/resistance levels.
    • Fundamental Analysis: Retrieval and presentation of company profiles, financial statements, key ratios, and valuation metrics from reliable sources like Yahoo Finance.
    • Macroeconomic Context: Incorporation of relevant macroeconomic data (e.g., interest rates, inflation) from sources like FRED to understand broader market influences.
  • Interactive Reporting: Generated reports are presented in a clear, interactive HTML format, allowing you to explore data and charts dynamically.
  • WordPress Automation: Seamless integration with WordPress for automated content generation and publishing, featuring customizable scheduling, author rotation, and adherence to your site's specific category and formatting requirements.

Our platform continuously evolves as we refine our models and incorporate new data sources to enhance accuracy and utility.
